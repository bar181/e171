Climate Action Around the World

The year 2015 marks a Milestone in Global Climate Action. 195 countries sign the Paris Agreement, agreeing to reduce Greenhouse Gas emissions. The goal is to limit warming to 1.5 degrees Celcius (2.7F). Every degree of warming matters.
