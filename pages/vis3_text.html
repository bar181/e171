The year 2015 marks a Milestone in Global Climate Action. 195 countries sign the Paris Agreement, agreeing to reduce Greenhouse Gas emissions. The goal is to limit warming to 1.5 degrees Celsius (2.7F). Every degree of warming matters.

Each nation's commitment to this agreement is a pivotal step towards a more sustainable future, and the collective effort is essential for driving significant change.
