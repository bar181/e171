What did you learn from Google Trends?

Did you remember when your Human Impact topics peaked in popularity?

Do you remember searching climate topics from 2004 to add to a Facebook post? Or in 2006 to write your first Tweet on Twitter? Or a climate image to post on Instagram in 2010?

For topics related to Human Impact it was interesting to see Activism and the Global Warming popular back when YouTube and Twitter were launched and Activism being popular again today

For topics related to Gas Emissions it was intriguing to observe that both Carbon Emissions and Greenhouse Gases surpassed the levels of interest they had when Facebook was first launched

For topics related to Global Warming it was interesting to see Renewable Energy stable in popularity since Instagram launched and others like Ecosystems gaining popularity

For topics related to Pollution and Nature it was interesting to see Habitats peak when YouTube started and the wild shift in popularity for Wildfires .

Emmett was surprise by the number of topics that are currently reaching their peak in popularity. In 2023, a total of 9 topics reached their highest levels of interest. Emmett wonders if the 2023 Heat Wave, Wildfires, Ocean Temperatures, and Government Cooperation played a role?
